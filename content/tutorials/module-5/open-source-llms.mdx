## Why teams choose open weights

- **Air-gapped** or regulated environments without third-party API egress.
- **Customization** — LoRA, full finetune, speculative decoding stacks.
- **Cost model** — capex hardware vs per-token opex tradeoff at high volume.

## Families you will meet

- **Llama** (Meta) — permissive-ish community license with usage restrictions; huge ecosystem.
- **Mistral / Mixtral** — strong European vendor + open drops.
- **Qwen, Gemma, Olmo** — research- and product-friendly variants with different terms.

## License literacy

“Open” ≠ **public domain**. Check **commercial use**, **attribution**, **export control**, and **redistribution** of derivatives. Legal + security sign-off before shipping customer features.

<Callout type="caution">
Downloading **GGUF** from random mirrors is like pulling **Docker images** from unverified registries — supply-chain risk. Prefer official org repos and **hash verification**.
</Callout>

## Key takeaways

- Open weights unlock **self-host + fine-tune** workflows.
- **License + safety + eval** are part of the technical decision.
- Treat checkpoints like **dependencies** — pin versions, scan provenance.
