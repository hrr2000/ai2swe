## Idea

Instead of updating full `W ∈ ℝ^{d×d}`, maintain:

```text
W' = W + ΔW,   ΔW = B A,   B ∈ ℝ^{d×r}, A ∈ ℝ^{r×d},   r ≪ d
```

Train only **A, B** per targeted layer (often `q_proj`, `v_proj`). **Merge** `ΔW` into `W` for deployment or keep adapters swappable.

## Why it works

Attention maps lie near **low intrinsic rank** for many task shifts — a few degrees of freedom steer behavior dramatically.

## Practical knobs

- **Rank `r`** — 8, 16, 64… trade quality vs size.
- **Alpha scaling** — ties effective learning rate of LoRA weights.
- **Target modules** — more modules = more capacity, more VRAM.

<Callout type="tip">
Use **Hugging Face PEFT** or **vLLM LoRA serving** to load multiple customer adapters behind one base GPU process — monetization pattern for B2B AI.
</Callout>

## Key takeaways

- LoRA = **low-rank trainable residuals** on frozen backbone.
- Dominant paradigm for **consumer GPU fine-tunes** of LLMs.
- Deployment can **merge** or **hot-swap** adapters — pick per release strategy.
