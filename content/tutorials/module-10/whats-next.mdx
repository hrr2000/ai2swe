## Model landscape

- **Frontier closed APIs** keep pushing multimodal + reasoning, while **open weights** chase efficiency with MoE and quantization.
- **Personal/local** models become good enough for **drafting + privacy** workloads.

## Systems engineering

- **Eval harnesses** become as standard as unit tests — `pytest` for prompts.
- **Cost controls** per tenant via caching, model routing, and feature flags.
- **Observability** merges classic APM with **LLM trace viewers**.

## Policy + trust

- **EU AI Act**, **copyright suits**, and **provenance standards** (C2PA) pressure vendors to disclose training data strategies.
- Enterprises demand **VPC**, **zero retention**, and **on-prem adapters**.

## For your career

Deepen **security mindset**, **data engineering**, and **product sense**. Raw transformer math is table stakes; **shipping reliable AI inside regulated environments** is the differentiator.

## Key takeaways

- Commoditization pushes value to **orchestration, evals, and compliance**.
- Agents + tools + RAG form the **default architecture**, not raw chat.
- Stay curious, stay skeptical, **measure everything**.
