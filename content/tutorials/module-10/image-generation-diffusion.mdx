## Forward noising process

Training samples images `x₀`, adds noise across schedule `t` → `T` until almost pure Gaussian. The network learns to **predict noise** (or velocity) to reverse the corruption.

## Sampling loop

Start from `x_T ~ Normal`, iteratively denoise with learned updates — dozens to hundreds of steps depending on scheduler (**DDIM**, **DPM-Solver++**, etc.).

## Guidance

**Classifier-free guidance** scales the conditional vs unconditional score difference — higher scale → **prompt lock-in** but **artifact risk** (oversaturated skin, extra limbs).

## Product concerns

- **Copyright** — training data provenance matters for enterprise adoption.
- **Latency** — real-time apps need distilled few-step models (SDXL Turbo, latent consistency models).
- **Safety filters** — NSFW classifiers on prompts and latents.

<Callout type="warning">
Generated **faces of real people** or **fake IDs** create fraud surfaces — policy + watermarking + provenance metadata (C2PA) are shipping requirements, not science fair extras.
</Callout>
